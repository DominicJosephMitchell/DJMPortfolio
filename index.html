After 6 six years of trouble shooting networks, I realized, I really loved working in tech, but, there was still something missing. I did some soul searching on what really makes me happy. Thats when it hit me to put all my skills, technology and experience together: building and maintaining the day-to-day operation of computer networks that companies and organizations rely on, aslong with web-design and development was the answer.

The best of all my worlds combined. I can design, create, build, lead/manage, work alone or in a team enviorment and I dont have to beatup/destroy my body doing it. As a previous Entrepreneur, Project Manager, and Leader I have multiple skills that transfer to the tech industry including leadership, organization, attention to detail, and perseverance through problem solving.
